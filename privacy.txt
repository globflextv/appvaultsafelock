Privacy policy
===========
General
-----------
App Vault-Safe Lock is developed by one main developer, with a few people helping from time to time, mostly with translations.

Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how information obtained from users is collected, used and disclosed.

By using App Vault-Safe Lock, you agree that your personal information will be handled as described in this Policy.

Information being collected

App Vault-Safe Lock does not collect any personal identifiable information.


The 'SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW' permission is used for asking user the pattern for locked applications.
The 'RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED' permission is used for starting App lock services on start.
The 'PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS' permission will check that ho many time this app is used.
The 'GET_TASKS' permission is used for checking that a locked app running or not (to prompt for password).
The 'RESTART_PACKAGES' permission is used for checking whether a locked app restarted (to prompt for password again).
